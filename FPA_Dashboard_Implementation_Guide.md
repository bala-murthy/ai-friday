# 1. Introduction

This guide explains how to build a production-grade FP&A (Financial Planning & Analysis) dashboard using:
- ChatGPT
- HTML
- JavaScript
- Interactive charts
- AI-assisted financial analytics

The dashboard helps organizations analyze:
- revenue performance
- margin trends
- quarterly targets
- forecast accuracy
- budget utilization
- opportunity pipeline health
- operational risks

---

# 2. What is FP&A Analytics

FP&A stands for Financial Planning and Analysis.

FP&A teams help leadership answer:
- Are we meeting revenue goals?
- Are budgets being utilized efficiently?
- Which opportunities are profitable?
- What risks exist in the pipeline?
- What is our quarterly forecast?

# 10. Risk Detection Rules

| Condition | Risk |
|---|---|
| Actual Revenue < 50% Planned | Revenue Risk |
| Margin < 10% | Profitability Risk |
| Budget > Revenue | Cost Risk |
| Failed High-Value Opportunity | Strategic Risk |
| Forecast Miss > 30% | Forecast Risk |
